Classic Pancakes

Step 1: In a bowl, mix 1 cup flour, 2 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
Step 2: In another bowl, whisk 1 cup milk, 1 egg, and 2 tbsp melted butter.
Step 3: Combine wet and dry ingredients. Stir until just mixed.
Step 4: Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Pour batter to form pancakes. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook until golden.
Step 5: Serve warm with your favorite toppings.
